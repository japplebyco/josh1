Weizenbier

Yum

Weizenbier is a kind of wheat beer that is exclusively made in southern Germany. This area of Germany is known as Bavaria, and weizenbiers have been brewed in this area for almost the last 1000 years. Franziskaner is my favorite weizenbier. I find this type of beer to be the most well balanced, when compared to most other types of American or European beers, most beers are more bitter and hoppy.

Reformed Theology

Interesting

History, philosophy, and theology have fascinated me from a very young age. Reformed Theology is one of the most complex and multifacited forms of Christian Theology that grew out of the protestant reformation about 500 years ago. The great revolutionaries of modern history are represented throughout the Reformed tradition by several monumental figures from Augustin to R.C. Sproul.

Josh Appleby
